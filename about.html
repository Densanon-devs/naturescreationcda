Our Story

Building Custom Homes Since 1993

Nature's Creation Contracting, Inc. "NCI" was established in 1993. Specializing exclusively in custom homes and luxury estates on Lake Coeur d'Alene and surrounding areas. Winners of the Prestigious Building Hand-In-Hand Award — working together to strengthen North Idaho's building industry, awarded by NIBCA.

In 2009 our company had the 1st ever full home renovation permitted in the Parade of Homes by the NIBCA, winning a prestigious award in every class.

Under the leadership of owner and general contractor Mike George, NCI has spent over three decades crafting homes that reflect the beauty and character of North Idaho and Eastern Washington. Mike is on-site daily from initial vision through completion, ensuring every detail meets the highest standard of quality.
