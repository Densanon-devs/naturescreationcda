Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often from prospective homeowners.

Scheduling a consultation is easy. You can call us directly at (208) 665-2120, email us at naturescreationcda@gmail.com, or fill out the contact form above. We'll set up a time to meet with you, discuss your vision, review your lot or property, and walk through the custom home building process from start to finish.

We proudly serve homeowners throughout North Idaho and Eastern Washington, including Coeur d'Alene, Post Falls, Hayden, and the surrounding Inland Northwest communities. If you're in the region and aren't sure whether we cover your area, give us a call — we're happy to discuss your project.

Custom home construction timelines vary based on size, complexity, and design features. A standard custom home typically takes 8 to 14 months from groundbreaking to move-in, while larger luxury estates may take 12 to 18 months or more. During your initial consultation, we'll provide a detailed project timeline tailored to your specific build so you know exactly what to expect at every stage.

You won't need to navigate the bureaucracy yourself. We ensure everything is built to code and properly documented at every phase of the project.

This means we typically build a select number of custom homes at a time, ensuring quality is never compromised.
