Why Choose Nature's Creation?

Since 1993, Mike George has been experienced in all aspects of new home construction in the Pacific Northwest. From building new homes to renovating existing ones, Nature's Creation Contracting delivers experience, knowledge, and design expertise on every project.

We purposefully limit ourselves to one or two projects per year so that owner Mike George can be on-site daily, ensuring every detail meets our exacting standards. This exclusive focus means your home receives the full attention it deserves from start to finish.

  • ✓ 30+ years of construction expertise
  • ✓ Owner on-site daily
  • ✓ Energy-efficient timeless designs
  • ✓ Exceeds state and local building codes

Our Process

From Vision to Completion

Building a custom home is a journey. We guide you through every phase with clear communication, expert craftsmanship, and unwavering attention to detail.

1

Initial Consultation

We sit down to understand your vision, lifestyle, and goals. Together we discuss your ideal home, preferred location, and budget to create a clear roadmap.

2

Design & Architecture

Working with premier architects, we develop detailed plans that capture your vision while incorporating energy-efficient and structurally superior design principles.

3

Expert Construction

With Mike George on-site daily, your home is built with meticulous care. We exceed building codes and use only premium materials and trusted subcontractors.

4

Final Walkthrough

We walk through every room and detail together, ensuring your new home meets the highest standards before we hand you the keys to your dream.
