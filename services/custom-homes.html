Custom Home Construction

Building dream homes on the shores of Lake Coeur d'Alene and throughout North Idaho since 1993.

Your Vision, Built to Last

At Nature's Creation Contracting, custom home building is more than a business — it's our craft. Since 1993, owner Mike George has been delivering one-of-a-kind homes throughout the Coeur d'Alene region, earning a reputation for uncompromising quality and attention to detail.

Our team is well versed in all aspects of construction and know how material lives and breathes in our Pacific Northwest environment. This deep understanding of regional building science means your home won't just look beautiful — it will perform flawlessly through decades of Idaho seasons, from heavy snow loads to summer heat.

Every custom home we build is designed around you — your lifestyle, your family's needs, and the unique character of your lot. Whether you're building on a lakefront parcel, a wooded hillside, or an established neighborhood, we bring the same energy-efficient building methods and premium craftsmanship to every project.

What We Offer

  • Site Selection & Land Acquisition Guidance

    We help you evaluate potential building sites for soil conditions, orientation, views, and buildability — so you make the right investment before design begins.

  • Custom Floor Plan Development

    Working with premier architects, we develop plans tailored to your lifestyle — from open-concept family living to multi-generational layouts and everything in between.

  • Foundation to Finish Construction

    Mike George is on-site daily overseeing every phase of construction. No detail is too small, no standard too high.

  • Energy-Efficient Building Methods

    Advanced insulation, high-performance windows, and modern HVAC systems ensure your home is comfortable year-round while keeping energy costs low.

  • Premium Material Selection

    We source the finest materials — from locally milled timber to imported stone and tile — choosing products that perform in the Pacific Northwest climate.

  • Code Compliance Exceeding Standards

    We don't just meet building codes — we exceed them. Every Nature's Creation home is built to standards that go well beyond minimum requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a custom home?

Most custom homes take between 10 and 18 months from groundbreaking to completion, depending on size, complexity, and site conditions. We provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase so you know exactly what to expect.

Do I need to have land before contacting you?

Not at all. We offer site selection and land acquisition guidance and can help you evaluate potential building sites throughout North Idaho and Eastern Washington before you purchase.

Can I make changes during construction?

We understand that ideas evolve. While we encourage finalizing plans before construction begins to avoid delays and cost increases, we work closely with you throughout the build and can accommodate reasonable changes when possible.

What makes building in North Idaho different?

Our Pacific Northwest climate presents unique challenges — heavy snow loads, freeze-thaw cycles, and variable humidity. With 30+ years of building in this region, we know how materials live and breathe here, and we design and build specifically for these conditions.
