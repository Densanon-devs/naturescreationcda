Our Services

From concept to completion, Nature's Creation delivers exceptional craftsmanship across every facet of custom home building in North Idaho and Eastern Washington.

Custom Home Construction

Every Nature's Creation home is built from the ground up to reflect your vision, lifestyle, and the unique character of the Pacific Northwest. With over 30 years of hands-on experience, we manage every detail from foundation to finish — delivering homes that are structurally superior, energy-efficient, and built to stand the test of time in our demanding climate.

Luxury Estates

For discerning clients seeking the finest in lakefront living, we limit ourselves to just one or two luxury estate projects per year. This ensures Mike George is on-site daily, collaborating with premier architects and using the highest-quality materials to create truly extraordinary residences on the shores of Lake Coeur d'Alene.

Home Renovations

Nature's Creation has a remarkable reputation for the amazing transformations they deliver on existing homes. From award-winning full-home renovations — including the first-ever full home renovation in the NIBCA Parade of Homes — to targeted kitchen, bathroom, and structural upgrades, we bring the same precision and care to renovations as we do to new construction.

Design Consultation

Great homes begin with great planning. We work alongside you and premier architects to develop a custom design that fits your desired function, aesthetic, and budget. From initial vision and land acquisition through material selection and permit coordination, our consultation process ensures your project is thoughtfully planned before construction ever begins.

Why Choose Nature's Creation

Since 1993, Nature's Creation Contracting has been the trusted name in custom home building throughout North Idaho and Eastern Washington. Owner Mike George brings over 30 years of hands-on construction expertise to every project — and unlike many builders, he's on-site daily ensuring every detail meets our exacting standards.

  • 30+ years of construction experience in the Pacific Northwest
  • Owner on-site daily — not delegated to project managers
  • Award-winning craftsmanship in every class
  • Deep knowledge of how materials live and breathe in our climate
  • Relationships with the region's premier architects and trades
  • Limited projects for maximum quality and attention

Our Process

Every project follows a proven four-step process refined over three decades of building exceptional homes.

01

Consultation

We begin with a thorough conversation about your vision, needs, budget, and timeline. Mike George personally meets with every client to understand exactly what you're looking for and how we can bring it to life.

02

Design & Architecture

Working with premier architects, we develop detailed plans that balance your desired function, design aesthetic, and cost parameters — ensuring every element is thoughtfully considered before construction begins.

03

Expert Construction

With Mike on-site daily, our skilled crews execute your project with precision and care. We use premium materials suited to the Pacific Northwest climate and hold every detail to the highest standard.

04

Final Walkthrough

Before handing you the keys, we conduct a comprehensive walkthrough together. Every system is tested, every finish inspected, and every detail confirmed — so you move into a home that exceeds your expectations.
