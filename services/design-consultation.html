Design Consultation

Where great homes begin — expert guidance from initial vision through every design decision.

From Vision to Blueprint

Every exceptional home starts long before the first nail is driven. At Nature's Creation Contracting, we believe the design consultation phase is the most important step in the entire building process — it's where your ideas become a detailed, buildable plan that fits your desired function, design aesthetic, and cost parameters.

With over 30 years of construction experience, Mike George brings a builder's perspective to the design process that most architects and designers simply can't provide. He understands what works in our Pacific Northwest climate, what materials perform best, and how design decisions impact both construction cost and long-term livability.

We work hand-in-hand with you and premier architects to develop custom homes that are not just beautiful on paper, but practical, buildable, and perfectly suited to your lifestyle. From the initial conversation about your vision through land acquisition, design development, material selection, and permit coordination — we guide you through every decision with expertise and care.

What We Offer

  • Initial Vision Planning

    We start by understanding how you live — your daily routines, entertaining style, family needs, and long-term plans. This forms the foundation for every design decision that follows.

  • Budget & Scope Development

    We help you establish a realistic budget and project scope early in the process, ensuring your design ambitions align with your investment parameters before architectural work begins.

  • Architect Collaboration

    We connect you with premier architects who specialize in the type of home you envision, then work alongside them throughout the design process to ensure plans are both stunning and buildable.

  • Material & Finish Selection

    From structural materials to interior finishes, we guide you through thousands of choices with expert recommendations based on durability, aesthetics, cost, and suitability for the Pacific Northwest climate.

  • Project Timeline Planning

    We develop a detailed construction timeline accounting for seasonal considerations, material lead times, and project complexity — so you have clear expectations from day one.

  • Permit Coordination

    Navigating building permits, zoning requirements, environmental regulations, and inspections can be complex. We manage the entire permitting process on your behalf, drawing on 30+ years of relationships with local agencies.

Our Process

01

Consultation

Mike George meets with you to understand your vision, lifestyle needs, and goals. We discuss sites, budgets, timelines, and what makes your dream home unique.

02

Design & Architecture

We partner with architects to translate your vision into detailed plans — iterating on layouts, elevations, and specifications until every element is exactly right.

03

Expert Construction

With plans finalized, our team brings the design to life with precision craftsmanship. Mike is on-site daily ensuring the built result matches the design intent perfectly.

04

Final Walkthrough

Together we inspect every detail of the completed home, verifying that the finished product matches — and exceeds — the vision we developed during consultation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to have a design in mind before the consultation?

Not at all. Some clients come to us with detailed Pinterest boards and magazine clippings, while others have only a general sense of what they want. Our consultation process is designed to help you discover and refine your vision, regardless of where you're starting from.

Can you work with my own architect?

Absolutely. While we have strong relationships with premier architects in the region and can make excellent recommendations, we're happy to collaborate with an architect you've already selected. The key is ensuring the design is buildable and suited to our climate.

How do you help manage project costs during design?

Cost management starts in the design phase. With 30+ years of building experience, Mike provides real-time cost feedback as design decisions are made — helping you understand the financial impact of choices before they're finalized. This prevents expensive surprises during construction.

What if I don't have land yet?

We can help with that too. Site selection is an important part of the design process, and we offer guidance on evaluating potential building sites — assessing factors like soil conditions, orientation, views, access, utilities, and buildability before you make a purchase.
