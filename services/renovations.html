Home Renovations

Award-winning home transformations that bring new life to existing homes throughout North Idaho.

Transforming Homes, Preserving Character

Nature's Creation Contracting has built a remarkable reputation for not only their new construction abilities, but the amazing transformations they're able to do to existing homes. Whether you've outgrown your current layout, want to modernize an aging property, or need to adapt your home for a new chapter of life, our renovation expertise delivers results that feel like an entirely new home.

In 2009, Nature's Creation made history by completing the first-ever full home renovation to be featured in the NIBCA Parade of Homes — and the result was award-winning. Since then, we've continued to earn recognition in every class we've entered, proving that our renovation work stands shoulder to shoulder with the finest new construction in the region.

Every renovation project receives the same hands-on attention as our custom homes. Mike George is on-site daily, ensuring that structural integrity, material quality, and finish work all meet the standard our clients have come to expect over our 30+ years of building in North Idaho.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I live in my home during a renovation?

It depends on the scope of the project. For targeted renovations like a kitchen or bathroom remodel, you can often remain in the home with some temporary adjustments. For full home renovations, we typically recommend arranging alternative living arrangements for the duration of construction.

How do you handle unexpected issues during renovation?

Older homes can reveal surprises once walls are opened. With 30+ years of renovation experience, we've encountered just about everything. We communicate findings immediately, present options, and work with you to address issues efficiently without compromising quality.

Is it better to renovate or build new?

This depends on several factors including the condition of the existing structure, your renovation goals, budget, and attachment to the property. During our initial consultation, Mike will give you an honest assessment of whether renovation or new construction best serves your needs.

How long does a typical renovation take?

Timelines vary significantly based on scope. A kitchen renovation might take 8 to 12 weeks, while a full home renovation can take 6 to 12 months. We provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase so you can plan accordingly.
